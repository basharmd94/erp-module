Formula of Depreciation Method:
Reducing Balance Method: Commonly Used
Net Book Value(WDV)* Depreciation Percent= Depreciation  per month
WDV=Asset cost – Total Depreciation 

Straight Line Method:
Amount – Salvage Value/Estimated Life=Depreciation  per month

Sum of the Year Digit Method:
WDV*Remaining Life/Total Sum of  Remaining Asset Life= Depreciation  per month
